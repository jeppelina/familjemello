# Tournament Rules — Familje-Mello

## The Format (16-song version)

### Overview
16 songs compete across 4 group stages. The best songs advance through Andra Chansen to the Grand Final, where one song is crowned the winner.

### Group Stages (Deltävlingar)

There are **4 groups** with **4 songs** each. Each group is its own mini-show.

For each group:
1. Watch all 4 songs together (YouTube videos)
2. Everyone votes (using the chosen points system)
3. Points are tallied and results revealed

**Results per group:**
- 🥇 **1st place → Direkt till final** (straight to the grand final)
- 🥈 **2nd place → Direkt till final**
- 🥉 **3rd place → Andra Chansen** (second chance)
- 4th place → **Eliminated** 😢

After all 4 groups: **8 songs** are in the final, **4 songs** go to Andra Chansen, **4 songs** are out.

### Andra Chansen (Second Chance)

The 4 "Andra Chansen" songs compete in **head-to-head duels:**

- Duel 1: Group 1's #3 vs Group 2's #3
- Duel 2: Group 3's #3 vs Group 4's #3

Watch both songs, then vote. **Winner of each duel advances to the final.**

After Andra Chansen: **10 songs** are in the final.

### Grand Final (Stor Final)

All 10 finalists compete. This is the big one!

1. Watch all 10 songs again (or clips/highlights)
2. Everyone votes using the **full Mello points system** (regardless of what was used in groups):
   - Each voter distributes: **12, 10, 8, 6, 4, 2, 1** points to their top 7 songs
   - Or use the simpler star system if preferred
3. Points are revealed dramatically — one voter at a time
4. The song with the most total points **wins Familje-Mello!** 🏆🎉

## Voting Systems Explained

### 🌟 Stars (Default — recommended for mixed ages)
Each person gives 1–5 stars to every song. Simple, fair, everyone understands it.

| Stars | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| ⭐ | Inte min grej (Not my thing) |
| ⭐⭐ | Okej (It's okay) |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | Bra! (Good!) |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Jättebra! (Really good!) |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | ÄLSKAR DEN! (LOVE IT!) |

### 👍 Simple (Great for the 4-year-old)
Three big buttons:
- 💗 = 3 points (Love it!)
- 😊 = 1 point (It's nice)
- 😴 = 0 points (Not for me)

### 🎯 Full Mello (For the grand final or competitive families)
Each voter has a set of points to distribute — they must give exactly one score to one song:

**7 voters or fewer:** 12, 10, 8, 6, 4, 2, 1
**Simplified version:** 5, 4, 3, 2, 1

You cannot give 12 to two songs — forces you to pick a real favorite!

## Special Awards (Optional, Phase 2)

- **🔍 Upptäckarpriset** (Discovery Award) — voted separately: "Which song surprised you the most?"
- **💃 Danspriset** (Dance Award) — "Which song made you dance the most?"
- **😂 Roligaste** (Funniest) — "Which song/performance was the funniest?"
- **👶 Lillas favorit** — The 4-year-old's personal pick (always celebrated!)

---

## Song Nomination Rules (Suggestions)

To make it a discovery experience, consider these house rules:

1. **Everyone nominates:** Each family member picks 3–4 songs
2. **The surprise rule:** At least 1 of your picks must be a song nobody else in the family has heard
3. **Genre stretch:** Try to pick from at least 2 different genres
4. **The wild card:** 1–2 songs are chosen randomly (e.g., a random Wikipedia "song of the day" or a song from a country you've never listened to music from)
5. **No repeats:** A song that's been in a previous Familje-Mello can't compete again (but can be in the "Hall of Fame playlist")

## Running a Familje-Mello Evening

### Suggested schedule (16 songs, ~2 hours):

| Time | What |
|------|------|
| 0:00 | 🎬 Opening! Welcome to Familje-Mello! (play the real Mello opening music?) |
| 0:05 | 🎵 Deltävling 1 (4 songs + voting) |
| 0:25 | 🍿 Break — snacks! |
| 0:30 | 🎵 Deltävling 2 (4 songs + voting) |
| 0:50 | 💃 Interval act — dance break or silly challenge |
| 0:55 | 🎵 Deltävling 3 (4 songs + voting) |
| 1:15 | 🍿 Break |
| 1:20 | 🎵 Deltävling 4 (4 songs + voting) |
| 1:40 | ⚔️ Andra Chansen (2 duels) |
| 1:50 | 🏆 GRAND FINAL (voting + dramatic reveal) |
| 2:00 | 🎉 Winner celebration! |

### Alternatively: spread over a weekend
- **Friday evening:** Deltävling 1 & 2
- **Saturday afternoon:** Deltävling 3 & 4 + Andra Chansen
- **Saturday evening / Sunday:** GRAND FINAL (make it an event — popcorn, decorations!)
